Instructions:

How To Use:

1) select face of object
2) click on slider(not to sure why its needed)
3) press either a,w,s,d, to rotate the object
4) press e,f to increase or decrease the radius
5) press auto rotate to make the cube rotate on its own over an axis.


Selection:
there is a white cube that shows what vertex is selected
j,k,l,I keys will move the selection cube around the grid
change height button will change what height will be input.
